About Me

Daniel Chi graduated from Cornell University in 2017 with a Bachelor of Arts. He is currently working as a tutor at C2 Education of Sugarloaf, teaching math, science, and language arts at the AP level, as well as SAT and ACT prep. He is also studying computer science under the GA Tech Coding Boot Camp program.

He grew up in Ohio before moving to Seoul, South Korea for three years ending in the 6th grade. He then spent another year in Ohio before moving down to Georgia, where he now resides.

On Sundays, he volunteers at Vision Presbyterian Church. In his spare time, he enjoys anime, video games, and "so bad it's good" movies. He also occasionally enjoys ice skating or bowling (high score of 180).
